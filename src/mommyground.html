Навчальний простір

Дошкільний заклад, який створює безпечні умови для дітей

Ivybrook Academy provides loving and educational Preschool programs for children between the ages of eighteen months and six years. In partnership with parents, we create a nurturing environment in which each child’s individual potential for social, emotional, physical, and intellectual growth is optimized and fulfilled.

Ivybrook Academy is open ten months of the year and operates a separate Summer Camp program for the remaining two months. Our hours of operation are Monday through Friday 8:15 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. during the school year and 9:00 a.m. to 12:00 p.m. during the summer programs. We offer both morning and afternoon sessions, and our half-days allow Ivybrook to maximize children’s hours in school with impactful learning experiences while maintaining a developmentally appropriate amount of time away from family. Half-days can be less stressful for children and help prepare them for full-day schooling.

Ми доклали чимало зусиль, щоб встановити найсучаснішу систему безпеки по всьому закладу.
